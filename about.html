Bio

Joyce Rice is a designer with a focus in journalism and education. She makes comics, games, and other digital products to help newsrooms and educators tell powerful stories.

Her work has been written about by Newsweek, Fast Company, Citylab, and others. She has spoken about media innovation and collaboration at SXSW, Game Developers Conference, on The Verge's Top Shelf, and elsewhere. Her past clients include PBS, Vox Media, Bed Bath & Beyond, and more.

Joyce Rice is also a cartoonist. During the summers, she is a lecturer at California College of the Arts where she teaches cartoonists and designers how to code so they can share their own digital stories.

She is currently accepting new clients and is available for speaking engagements. If you'd like to work together, get in touch with more information about your project!
